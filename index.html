Who we are

WE BUILD
THE FUTURE

Secondlaw is a design and engineering studio founded on a simple principle: objects should be honest. Honest about how they're made, what they do, and what they say about the people who use them.

We operate at the intersection of industrial design, mechanical engineering, and rapid fabrication. Our work spans consumer products, medical devices, industrial equipment, and experimental concepts — all united by a commitment to precise thinking and bold form.
